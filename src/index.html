Meet Frank.
He’s who launched
Thoughts Around Me.


Frank’s a curious little guy who wanted to bring everyone together through
thoughts, conversations and community. That’s why he decided to launch
Thoughts Around Me.



Connect with
people around you

Wherever you are, you’ll always be surrounded by opinions. With
Thoughts Around Me you get real-time access to what’s happen-
ing around you. Whether it’s an opinion on David Cameron’s deci-
sion making skills or an argument over Shoreditch’s latest hipster
establishment, you’ll find it right here on Thoughts Around Me.

Discover
new communities

Think you’re the only person interested in the linguistic structure
of the fictional languages in Game of Thrones? Franks begs to
differ. Thoughts Around Me’s groups cater to your interests, in
whatever form they may come. Join existing groups to share your
thoughts on specific topics or create a new group and start grow-
ing your community.

Share your
thoughts, privately

Thoughts Around Me is completely anonymous. That means no
profiles and no passwords, ever. Your thoughts and opinions are
safe with us, giving you the freedom to express yourself. But hey,
respect the house rules, Frank doesn’t accept foul play.
